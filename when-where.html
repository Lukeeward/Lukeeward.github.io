When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Saturday, 29th June, 2019, 2.00 PM - 4.00PM @ The Pilkington Cob Shelter, RHS Rosemoor

Join us for our outdoor ceremony at The Pilkington Cob Shelter. Please arrive by 1:15pm: at the visitors reception you will be provided a map and directions. If all else fails, follow the fanciest looking hat you can see! Rain or shine please bring an umbrella to protect yourself whatever the British weather throws our way.

Reception

Saturday, 29th June, 2019, 4.00 PM - 11.00PM @ The Garden Room, RHS Rosemoor

Following a drinks reception in the Winter Garden, we will continue into the newly built Garden Room for our wedding breakfast and reception. The evening reception will begin at 6:30pm till late, including live entertainment.


Before the ceremony

As guests you will have full entry to the RHS gardens, we think they are truly beautiful, so if you would like to have a good look around we would recommend coming along a bit earlier on the day.

Evening

The evening reception will start from 6:30pm, including live music and a BBQ on the terrace.

RHS Garden Rosemoor, Torrington, EX38 8PH

Are You Attending?

Please let us know by emailing Altria-Ward-Wedding@Outlook.com by Wednesday 13th March, including any dietary or other requirements for the day.

We hope you can make it.
