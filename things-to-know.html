Our Surname

If you know us well, you'll know we aren't a traditional couple, so we don't always do things in the traditional way. We have always prioritised equality in our relationship and it is for this reason that we have decided to take a shared name. We want to let you know, on the 29th of June, we will become Mr & Mrs Altria-Ward.

Wedding gifts

Your attendance on the day is more important to us than any gift. However, if you would really like to get us something, we are currently saving to buy a home to call our own. Any contribution would be gratefully appreciated!

Parking

Please park in the normal RHS rosemoor visitor carpark and go to the main reception for directions to the ceremony.

Confetti

Unfortunately, we are not allowed traditional confetti at the venue. So we will provide an alternative on the day - no need to bring your own!

The weather

Knowing the British weather, we recommend that you prepare for whatever it may bring. Please be aware that a significant portion of the day will be outside making the most of the gardens. There is a chance that it could be very hot so please ensure you have plenty of suncream and an umbrella for shade (or rain!).

Cash bar

There will be a cash bar available in the evening, if you would like to use this please make sure you bring cash along on the day.

Photos & Social Media

It's far more important to us that you enjoy the day in the moment than worry about getting the perfect photo. For this reason, we ask that you do not take photos during the ceremony. If you wish to take photos after the ceremony you are more than welcome, however, we ask that you do not upload these to social media. If you'd like to share photos you've taken you can do so here, the password is ES8d5be581.
